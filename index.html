Our Features

At the heart of this tool lies simplicity and effectiveness, empowering you to develop a skillset that’s often underestimated — reading efficiency.

Confidence Through Reading

Improving your reading speed isn't just about faster consumption of text; it's about building confidence in your ability to tackle new information, grasp complex topics, and feel empowered in both personal and professional settings. Mastering reading efficiency boosts overall confidence in learning, allowing you to approach materials with greater ease.

Know Your Strengths

Understanding your current reading ability can be a powerful tool. This test helps you track your progress, uncover your strengths, and identify areas where you might improve, all while boosting your comprehension — not just speed.

Real-Time Reading Speed Calculation

Our test captures your reading speed accurately and provides insightful statistics on your progress. See how your average speed improves over time and track your journey as you become a more efficient reader.

Comprehension Questions

Because reading isn't just about speed, we've included comprehension questions to make sure you’re absorbing what you read. These questions are tailored to the passages, so you get a holistic measure of your reading ability.

Personalized Settings

Make your reading experience truly yours with customizable font sizes and styles. We want to ensure the experience is comfortable for you, so you can focus on the content rather than the interface.

Track Your Progress

Monitor your improvement across multiple sessions. The system keeps a log of your past speeds and comprehension scores, giving you tangible data on how far you’ve come.

About This Project

We believe that everyone can improve their reading skills, no matter their starting point. This project was built with a simple goal in mind: to help people read better, faster, and with greater comprehension.

Why We Created This Tool

We wanted to create something practical yet approachable, allowing anyone to gauge and improve their reading efficiency. Whether you’re a student, a professional, or simply someone looking to enjoy reading more, this tool can make a difference. Developed with a humble vision, this project is meant to be a resource that grows with you.

Our Vision for Growth

Our goal is to keep expanding the tool, adding new features, and listening to feedback from users like you. Reading is an ever-evolving skill, and we aim to be part of your journey, helping you develop not just speed but also comprehension and confidence.

Committed to Your Success

We’re not here to sell a promise of overnight results. True progress comes from consistent practice, and that’s where this tool shines. We hope to be the quiet yet reliable support you need to keep improving, little by little.
